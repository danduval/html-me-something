About the Author

I have been a musician since childhood. Piano lessons, garage bands, community theater orchestra pits; I explored the usual range of musical outlets available to a young person growing up in the suburbs of San Jose, California until graduating from high school. After school, I moved to Boston and studied at Berklee College of Music before moving back west and finishing a music degree at PSU.

Since college I've been living in Portland, playing in lots of bands, teaching guitar lessons, releasing albums, and working in the service industry on the side. I've been fortunate to travel for musical reasons from time to time, although I only got overseas that way once, so far. Spending two weeks playing small shows in Belgium and Paris was one of the highlights of my decade of post-collegiate music.
